📖 How to Play

1
Read the student totals

Each class shows how many students it has.

2
Add footballs to class bars

Click + Add to add a ⚽ to a column.

3
Each ⚽ = 10 students

So 3 footballs = 30 students!

💡 Half football ◐ = 5 students (for odd numbers)

4
Check your pictograph

Click "Check My Pictograph" to verify.

5
Answer reasoning questions

Solve 5 questions about your pictograph.

6
Learn from mistakes

If wrong, the helper shows step-by-step solutions!

Example:

Class 8 = 40 students → ⚽⚽⚽⚽ (4 × 10 = 40)
